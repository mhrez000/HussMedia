The Approach

Storytelling, Not Just Photos

A wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime story, and it deserves to be told beautifully. I blend candid documentary moments with editorial portraits and a cinematic highlight film — so decades from now you'll relive not just how the day looked, but how it felt.

Calm, unobtrusive, and detail-obsessed on the day; warm and easy to work with leading up to it. From the first look to the last dance, I'm there to capture all of it.
